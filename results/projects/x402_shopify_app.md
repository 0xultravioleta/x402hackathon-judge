## Project Description
A Shopify app that enables merchants to accept crypto payments through the x402 v2 protocol. Store owners install the app, configure their wallet address and accepted tokens, then get an instant checkout page at store.myshopify.com/apps/x402-checkout where customers or AI agents can browse products and pay with crypto wallets.
